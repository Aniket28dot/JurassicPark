About Us

Jurassic Park is Asia's Craziest Theme Park located at New Town Gurgram. This is the perfect place to spend your holidays with your family and experience the thrill. The Park recieves nearly 20,000 visitors daily from different parts of the world. All the rides are equipped with the best of safety measures to ensure the safety of all the visitors and provide them an awefull experience without any kind of mischieve. The food stalls are located at each corner inside the park to satisfy the hunger of our visitors. Laser Show and the Horror Nights are the most cheered events at the Jurassic Park and experience a footfall of nearly 100K people every year from all around the globe. The Play rooms will keep your child engaged with different art and creative activities. Tickets are available on the counters and also on the official site of the Jurassic Park. No Tickets for children below 5 years of age. Wishing You a Thrilling Experience.
